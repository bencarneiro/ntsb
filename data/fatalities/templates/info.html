Our Mission

We believe that behind every traffic fatality statistic is a human story—a life cut short by preventable engineering failures and policy choices that prioritize speed over safety.

roadway.report serves as both a memorial to those we've lost and a call to action for systemic change in how we design and build our transportation infrastructure.

"Every dot on our map represents someone's parent, child, sibling, or friend—a life that mattered."

🗺️
The Platform

Our interactive web map visualizes every traffic death across the United States, allowing users to explore the human impact of dangerous street design and policy decisions.

Each incident is mapped with comprehensive federal data, creating a powerful tool for understanding patterns, identifying dangerous locations, and advocating for evidence-based safety improvements.

This isn't just data—it's a testament to the urgent need for Vision Zero policies and human-centered street design.
